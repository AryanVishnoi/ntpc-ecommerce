About NTPC

National Thermal Power Corporation Limited (NTPC) is India's largest power utility, contributing over 25% of the nation's power requirement with an installed capacity of more than 70 GW.

As part of our digital transformation journey, we present the NTPC e-Procurement Platform - a centralized digital solution designed to revolutionize how we manage consumables and supplies across all our units.

This platform represents our commitment to operational excellence through technology adoption.

Our Mission & Purpose

Streamlined Procurement

To create a seamless procurement ecosystem that connects all NTPC units, ensuring timely availability of consumables while optimizing costs and reducing redundancy.

Core Values

Built on the pillars of transparency, accountability, efficiency, and sustainability - reflecting NTPC's commitment to responsible business practices.

What This Platform Offers

A comprehensive digital solution designed specifically for NTPC's procurement needs

Extensive Catalog

Access to various categories including safety gear, office supplies, electrical parts, and more - all standardized for NTPC requirements.

Seamless Transactions

Intuitive workflows for request, issue, return, and reporting - all integrated with NTPC's approval systems.

Real-time Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards and reports for inventory tracking, consumption patterns, and cost optimization.

Designed For NTPC Teams

This portal is specifically designed to serve the needs of various stakeholders within the NTPC ecosystem:

  • Employees across all NTPC units for consumable requests
  • Storekeepers for inventory management and distribution
  • Department heads for approvals and budget tracking
  • Administrators for system configuration and reporting

Our Vision for the Future

Expansion of Product Categories

Continuous addition of new product categories to cover all procurement needs across NTPC operations.

System Integration

Deeper integration with other NTPC systems like ERP, finance, and HR for end-to-end process automation.

Smart Inventory

Implementation of AI-powered inventory management with predictive analytics for optimal stock levels.
